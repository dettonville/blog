About Infra Coder

Mar 02, 2022

What is Infra Coder? Infra Coder is the side projects and after hour throughts and interests of a datacenter automation coder. So InfraCoder will be quite variable in both content, tools and platforms used. This site blog is used to share code and ideas and to link to other web content.

The author, Lee Johnson, is a CICD Infrastruction Automation Architect/Engineer working as a consultant. He has been working as a infrastructure automation projects for almost 10 years and for the last couple has been leading a small but remarkable engineering team working predominantly with Openshift.

All views, opinions and code shared here are the authors. This is a personal not a corporate blog.

Please feel free to reach out via the comments section below, or in any post. Feed back and discussion is welcome.
